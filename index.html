Common Questions

FAQ

What does Adriana Martinkevica do?

Adriana is an education researcher and PhD candidate in Educational Leadership at the University of Latvia, researching teacher shortages and education policy. She also works as a practitioner on AI in education — speaking at conferences, running workshops for schools, and advising education organizations and policymakers.

How do I book Adriana to speak at a conference or event?

Use the contact form below and select "Speaking" as the inquiry type, or email hello@adrianamartinkevica.com directly with your event details, dates and audience size.

Does Adriana run workshops for schools?

Yes — half-day and full-day in-person workshops for schools and teacher teams on AI literacy, AI-assisted lesson planning, and CLIL/digital pedagogy training, starting from €600 per session. See Work with Adriana for details.

What is the Teacher Attractiveness Index?

A comparative research framework Adriana developed that compares entry conditions, retention systems, workload and policy stability across six countries. It's presented in "Defining Education Policy for the Teaching Profession", published in the European Journal of Educational Research (2026).
