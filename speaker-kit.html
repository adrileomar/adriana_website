Bio

Adriana Martinkevica is a PhD candidate in Educational Leadership at the University of Latvia and an AI-in-education practitioner. Her doctoral research examines the relationship between education policy and teacher shortages, drawing on comparative analysis across six countries. She developed the Teacher Attractiveness Index, published in the peer-reviewed European Journal of Educational Research, and has presented her research at EDULEARN26 (Palma, Spain) and the International Academic Conference on Education, Teaching and Learning (Helsinki, Finland). She also teaches English and coaches national and international debate teams of 70+ students.
